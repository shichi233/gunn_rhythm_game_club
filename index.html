    About the Club

    We’re a student-run community at Gunn HS dedicated to rhythm games—casual sessions, skill clinics, and friendly tourneys. All Gunn students are welcome.

    What we play

    osu! • Project SEKAI • Phigros • Taiko • Cytus/DEEMO • Muse Dash • DDR/ITG • beatmania IIDX • Sound Voltex • maimai — and more by request. All are welcome.

    Meetings

    Every Wednesday at lunch in C-6. Exact dates appear in the Events section. Bring your own device or use our club setups.

    What to expect

    Mostly we play together and help everyone unlock higher-level songs. We still do warm-ups and free play, and we’re happy to help beginners get started.

    Why join?

    • Learn timing, accuracy, and reading skills from friendly members.
    • Access club gear and curated practice charts.
    • Compete in on-campus brackets and online events.
    • Make friends who love music + games.

    FAQ

    Do I need experience?

    Nope! Beginners are welcome. We’ll teach core skills and help you pick charts that fit your level.

    What should I bring?

    A charged laptop/tablet/iPad/phone if you have one. We’ll also have shared setups when possible.

    Can non-members attend?

    Yes—Gunn students are welcome to drop in. If you enjoy it, submit the interest form to stay updated.
